Gomoa Fetteh Beach, Ghana

Gomoa Fetteh is a coastal town in the Central Region of Ghana, Located of the Akosti Junction on the Accra-Winneba highway. It is generally known in local circles for the fishing and subsistence farming activities of its people/ But it is the long clean stretch of the beach that causes people to visit Gomoa Fetteh.
